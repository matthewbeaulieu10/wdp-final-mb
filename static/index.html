Flyer Football Dispatch

Hello and welcome to the flyer dispatch! This application was intended to be an upgrade of the Lewis Men's Soccer website in comparison to the standard NCAA template that most school's use for their athletic programs. Right now this page has only very basic functionality. On the menu bar above us, you can (for the moment) edit the roster or schedule within the mongodb atlas databse, or you can also view the data that is stored their. The buttons above will take you to whatever path you desire to follow.
